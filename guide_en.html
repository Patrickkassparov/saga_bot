Tips

What increases
your chances

⚡

Speed is everything

SAGA receives hundreds of applications within hours. The bot applies automatically as soon as a listing appears — that's your decisive advantage over everyone else.

✅

Complete your Immomio profile 100%

Fill in all fields: personal info, income, pets, smoking. Add a profile photo. An incomplete profile means automatic rejection before a human even looks.

📋

SCHUFA — recommended

Upload your SCHUFA to your Immomio profile. Get it for free once a year: meineschufa.de → "Datenkopie nach Art. 15 DSGVO". Arrives by post in 1–2 weeks. A profile without SCHUFA looks incomplete.

📊

WBS — important for social housing

The Wohnberechtigungsschein gives access to subsidised SAGA apartments at lower rents. More than 40% of Hamburg households are eligible. Apply at: hamburg.de/wbs. Valid for 1 year, costs €9–20.

🔴

Dringlichkeitsbestätigung — boosts chances in crisis situations

An official document for people in genuine housing crisis: imminent homelessness, eviction, serious social circumstances. Issued by the district Fachstellen für Wohnungsnotfälle or Fachamt Grundsicherung und Soziales. Those who have it are given priority by SAGA.

📞

Behaviour at the viewing

Arrive 5–10 minutes early. Ask 1–2 practical questions (heating type, utilities). Don't criticise the apartment. At the end say: "Thank you for the viewing — I'm very interested." Bring your documents.

🎯

Realistic filters

Wider price and room range = more apartments = more chances. You can always skip individual apartments with "Not interested" in the bot.

Common questions

FAQ

Do I need an Immomio account?
Yes, absolutely. Immomio is the platform SAGA Hamburg uses for all apartment applications. Registration is free at tenant.immomio.com. Fill your profile 100% and upload all documents — then the bot can send applications on your behalf.
Where do I get a free SCHUFA?
meineschufa.de offers a free "Datenkopie nach Art. 15 DSGVO" — once per year. Order it in advance; it arrives by post in 1–2 weeks. You can also order an instant paid online report if you're in a hurry.
What is WBS and do I need it?
WBS (Wohnberechtigungsschein) is a permit for social housing in Germany. Some SAGA apartments are only available with a WBS and have reduced rents. If your income is below a certain threshold, apply at hamburg.de/wbs. Without WBS you can still search — most SAGA apartments have no restrictions.
Is it safe to enter my password in the bot?
Your password is stored encrypted (Fernet AES-128). Nobody has access to your account except for automated bot actions: submitting applications and accepting viewing invitations.
How quickly do viewing invitations arrive?
It varies — from a few days to several months. It depends on competition for that specific apartment. The most important thing is to be among the first to apply. The bot ensures this speed automatically.
